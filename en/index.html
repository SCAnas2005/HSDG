About the Event

About the Event

The HDS Digital Games Festival returns in 2025 for its third edition, orchestrated by the Hauts-de-Seine department. This major event will bring together over 60 influential figures from the video game, image, graphic arts and digital industries. Over 150 gaming stations will invite you to explore the latest innovations in gaming. Dive into 70 interactive workshops dedicated to themes such as artificial intelligence, virtual reality, drones, robotics, coding and digital media. This year, we're putting a special emphasis on education, with an area dedicated to educational and sports video games, as well as an awareness zone to understand the positive impacts and challenges of the gaming sector. Join us to explore the fascinating future of video games and technology, talk to passionate experts, and discover how these fields influence our daily lives and our future.

F.A.Q

  • What flagship activities will be present at the HDS Digital Games 2025 festival?

    The festival will feature video game demonstrations, artificial intelligence workshops, virtual reality experiences, coding sessions, drone competitions, exhibitions of digital and graphic arts, as well as educational and sports areas.

  • What types of video games will be available for visitors?

    You'll be able to try out a wide variety of games, ranging from classic video games to the latest releases, including educational games, sports games, virtual reality experiences, and indie games created by local developers.

  • Are interactive workshops free?

    Yes, all interactive workshops are included in the festival admission price. You can sign up on-site to participate in the various workshops offered.

  • How can I participate in video game competitions during the festival?

    You can register for competitions directly on our website or at the festival's reception on the day of the event. Competitions for different skill levels will be organized.

  • Will there be speakers or expert panels?

    Yes, the festival will host speakers and industry experts from the video game and digital sectors who will lead panels and discussions on various topics such as artificial intelligence, virtual reality, and current industry trends.

  • What security measures will be in place for participants?

    We are implementing strict security measures, including entry controls, video surveillance, and the presence of security personnel on-site. A health protocol will also be followed to ensure the safety of all participants.
